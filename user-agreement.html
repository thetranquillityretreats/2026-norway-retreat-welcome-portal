Who This Agreement Is Between

This agreement is made between The Tranquillity Retreats (“we,” “us,” “our”) and you, the participant who has registered for the Norway 2026 Retreat (the “Retreat”).

2

About the Retreat Experience

The Retreat takes place from 5 to 9 October 2026 at Panorama Hotel & Resort, Steinsland, Norway.

The Tranquillity Retreat is a five-day immersion into inner exploration, drawing from mystery school teachings, breathwork, sound healing, energy work, and consciousness expansion practices. This is not a wellness holiday or a surface-level relaxation experience. It is a deliberate journey through layers of self-discovery, ancestral connection, and expanded awareness.

The teachings offered draw from mystery school traditions, new age and metaphysical philosophy, and advanced practices of consciousness expansion, including material that may challenge conventional beliefs and invite profound personal reflection. This retreat is best suited for individuals with an open mind toward spiritual and new age information. If you feel called to explore expanded consciousness and soul-level understanding, you are exactly where you need to be.

Our facilitation team creates a safe, held space for this work. Every session is designed with intention, pacing, and integration in mind. The Retreat may include, but is not limited to, guided breathwork and breathing exercises, sound healing and gong ceremonies, fire ceremony, meditation, visualisation and somatic practices, movement and embodied practices, group workshops and reflective sessions, a Norway excursion in nature, and community sharing in sacred space. The specific programme may evolve to honour what the group needs. We will always be transparent with you about any changes.

3

What You May Experience

During the Retreat, it is completely natural to notice a range of physical, emotional, and energetic responses, including emotional releases such as tears, laughter, or the surfacing of old memories; physical sensations such as tingling, warmth, or involuntary movement; deep states of relaxation or heightened awareness; vivid inner imagery or moments of profound clarity; and temporary fatigue or heightened energy following sessions. These responses are a natural part of the process. Our facilitation team is trained to support you through them. You are never alone in this.

4

Your Rights During the Retreat

You may step out of any session at any time, for any reason. Simply stand and leave quietly and a facilitator will check on you. You are always encouraged to communicate with the facilitation team if anything feels uncomfortable or overwhelming. Integration sessions are built into the schedule to help you process and ground what surfaces.

In joining us, you agree to treat all participants, facilitators, and supporting team with care and consideration throughout the Retreat. What is shared by participants during sessions remains within the group. Your Health and Wellbeing

Taking full ownership of your experience is at the heart of Principle VI: Sovereignty. Your commitment to honesty in this section is an expression of that principle.

Your safety is our deepest priority. The practices we offer, including breathwork, ceremonial work, and deep meditative states, can create profound shifts physically, emotionally, and energetically. These experiences are by design, and they are held with care.

The facilitators of The Tranquillity are not licensed medical or mental health professionals. This Retreat is a wellness and personal development experience. It is not a substitute for professional medical or psychological care. If you are currently taking prescribed medication, please continue to do so as directed by your healthcare provider.

We ask that you complete the Health Intake Form honestly and in full. This is a separate form sent alongside this agreement. It allows our facilitation team to understand your history and ensure that every practice offered is appropriate for you. By signing this agreement, you confirm that you have disclosed all relevant medical, psychiatric, and physical health information in your Health Intake Form; that you have consulted a qualified medical professional if you have any concerns about your ability to participate safely; and that you accept personal responsibility for your wellbeing in relation to the practices offered. If your circumstances change after submitting your Health Intake Form, please contact us at retreats@thetranquillity.co.uk before the Retreat begins.

In the event of injury or illness during the Retreat, you consent to emergency medical assistance being sought on your behalf. You acknowledge that you are solely responsible for any associated costs, and that The Tranquillity makes no representations about the availability or outcome of any such treatment.

Capturing the Journey Together

Documenting and sharing the spirit of this work is one of The Tranquillity’s core principles. You can read more in Principle IX: We Are Vehicles of Change.

At The Tranquillity, we document our retreats with love and intentionality. Photographs and short videos are taken throughout our time together to honour the journey and share its spirit with the wider community. Documentation is woven into the fabric of the experience, not separate from it.

For any content that is both clearly identifiable and captures something of a personal, intimate nature consistent with the depth of work we do here, we will always reach out to you personally before publishing. This is our commitment to every participant, regardless of the preference indicated below.

We also ask that all participants extend this same care to one another. Please do not photograph or film fellow participants during sessions or ceremonies, and always ask before sharing images of others on social media. Please do not use any Retreat content for commercial purposes.

Content already published in good faith prior to any request cannot be recalled or removed. If your preference changes at any point during the Retreat, simply let a facilitator know. Raw media is retained for 24 months, after which it is securely deleted unless it has been published with prior consent.

If you would prefer not to be included in Retreat photography, please let us know on arrival and our team will provide you with a discreet visual identifier so every member of our documentation team knows to keep you out of frame.

Reference Document

Media Consent

At The Tranquillity, we document our retreats with love and intentionality. This document explains in full how that works — what we capture, how we use it, what protections are in place for you, and what rights you hold at every stage. Please read it before completing your onboarding form. It is here for you to return to at any time.

Definitions & Consent

Section 1
1

Definitions

Content Categories

Two types of content may be captured during the retreat.

Atmosphere Content is photography, video, and audio that captures the general feel of our time together — the landscape, shared meals, group moments, and the spirit of the space as it unfolds.

Session Content is footage captured during workshops, breathwork, ceremony, meditation, or sound healing — moments where you may be in a deeply open, inward, or emotionally vulnerable state.

Understanding this distinction is at the heart of how we approach everything else in this document.

Section 2
2

Consent to Atmosphere Content

General Retreat Coverage

If you selected Yes in the onboarding form, you are giving The Tranquillity permission to capture, use, and publish Atmosphere Content that may include your likeness. This may be used across our website, social media, YouTube, and other materials — to share the spirit of what we do with those who have not yet found us, and to honour the journey of those who have.

Section 3
3

Session Content Restrictions

Protecting Vulnerable Moments

Wide-angle or non-identifiable footage from sessions may be used as Atmosphere Content under the general consent above. Any footage that shows you in a deeply personal or emotionally open moment is held to a different and higher standard.

If our team feels that a moment has been captured that is truly special and you are recognisably in it, we will come to you personally after the retreat. We will share exactly what we have, explain where we would love to use it, and ask for your written sign-off before anything happens. You are never obligated to say yes.

This protection applies to every participant, regardless of which consent option they selected in the onboarding form. It is not conditional. It does not expire. It is simply how we work.

Universal Protection

Your vulnerability is sacred.

There is no version of this retreat where a moment of real tenderness, release, or breakthrough is used without you knowing about it first and saying yes. If we feel something truly special has been captured and you are in it, we will come to you personally — not through a form, but as a team reaching out to a person. We will share exactly what we have, ask if you are happy for it to be used, and honour your answer completely.

That is a promise we make to everyone who walks through the door.

Your Rights & Boundaries

Section 4
4

Right to Opt Out of Specific Captures

Your Boundaries in the Moment

If at any point during the retreat you would prefer not to be filmed or photographed in a specific session, simply let a facilitator know before it begins. We will do everything we reasonably can to honour that. This does not affect your general consent for Atmosphere Content, and you will never need to explain yourself.

Section 5
5

Participant-Captured Media

Your Own Photographs and Videos

You are very welcome to take your own photographs and videos during free time, meals, and social moments throughout the retreat. During facilitated sessions — breathwork, ceremony, meditation, and workshops — please keep your phone away unless a facilitator has specifically invited otherwise. This protects the space for everyone in it, including you.

Section 6
6

Social Media and Tagging

How We Reference You Online

We will never tag you individually in a social media post without asking you first. Group photographs shared online will not include individual name tags unless every person shown has confirmed they are happy with that. If you would like to be tagged, simply let us know and we will always be glad to.

Section 7
7

Testimonials

Sharing Your Experience

Your onboarding form invited you to share your preferences around testimonials. This section explains how that process actually works so that you know exactly what to expect.

If this retreat moves you and you feel called to share your experience, we will always handle your words with the same care we bring to everything else. Before any testimonial is used in a specific piece of content, we will reach out to you directly, share exactly what we have in mind, and ask for your confirmation. Your name will never appear alongside your words without your explicit permission. If you prefer to share anonymously, we will honour that completely.

Pre-consent given in the onboarding form is your indication of willingness. Final confirmation always happens after the retreat, before anything goes live. There is never any pressure, and never any expectation.

Data & Legal

Section 8
8

Third-Party Sharing

Who Has Access to Your Image

Media captured during the retreat will never be sold, licensed, or shared with any outside party. The only exception is professional editors and production personnel working directly under contract with The Tranquillity, solely for the purpose of producing retreat content. Your image belongs to this space and this community.

Section 9
9

Raw Media Retention

How Long We Keep Unedited Footage

Unedited footage and photographs are kept for a maximum of 60 months from the date of the retreat. After that period, anything not incorporated into published materials is permanently deleted. Published materials may remain in use indefinitely under the terms of this consent.

Section 10
10

Revocation of Consent

Withdrawing Your Approval

You may withdraw your consent to future use of your likeness at any time by writing to us at retreats@thetranquillity.co.uk. Withdrawal applies to future use only. Content already published before we receive your request cannot be recalled, as it was shared in good faith under your original consent. We will always act on a withdrawal notice promptly and with care.

Section 11
11

No Compensation

Use of Likeness

There is no financial payment for use of your likeness under this consent. The retreat experience and everything it holds is the full consideration.

Section 12
12

Governing Law

Jurisdiction

This consent is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising from it fall within the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

Modifications

Changes to This Document

If we need to update this document before the retreat begins, we will let you know directly. If you are not comfortable with any change, you are free to withdraw your consent at any time.

Media Consent

The Tranquillity Retreats is committed to capturing the spirit and atmosphere of our retreats while respecting the dignity, privacy, and emotional safety of every participant.

Definitions & Consent

1
Definitions
Content Categories

Atmosphere Content refers to general footage, photography, and audio recordings that capture the overall environment, scenery, group activities, and ambience of the retreat.

Session Content refers to footage, photography, or audio captured during workshops, ceremonies, breathwork, meditation, sound healing, or any facilitated session where participants may be in a heightened emotional, spiritual, or vulnerable state.

2
Consent to Atmosphere Content
General Event Coverage

If you have selected Yes in the onboarding form, you grant The Tranquillity Retreats permission to capture, use, and publish Atmosphere Content that may include your likeness for promotional, educational, and marketing purposes across all media channels including but not limited to websites, social media, print materials, and video platforms.

3
Session Content Restrictions
Protecting Vulnerable Moments

Session Content that does not identify individual participants (wide shots, silhouettes, non-identifiable footage) may be used as Atmosphere Content under the general consent above.

Session Content showing participants in emotionally vulnerable states will not be used without explicit individual written consent for the specific piece of content. This protection applies to all participants regardless of the option selected in the onboarding form.

Universal Protection

Your vulnerability is sacred.

If the team feels a sensitive moment has been captured and may be of value, we will approach you directly after the retreat, share the specific content with you, and request your explicit written permission before any use is made.

You are under no obligation to approve it. No vulnerable moment will ever be used without your individual post-retreat sign-off, regardless of which consent option you selected.

We will always treat it that way.

Your Rights & Boundaries

4
Right to Opt Out of Specific Captures
Your Boundaries in the Moment

You may request to not be filmed or photographed during any specific session by informing a facilitator before that session begins. The production team will make reasonable efforts to honour your request. Opting out of specific captures does not revoke your general consent for Atmosphere Content.

5
Testimonial Exclusion
Testimonials Require Separate Consent

This Media Consent does not cover testimonials. Any recorded testimonials, written reviews, or spoken endorsements require separate consent under The Tranquillity Retreats’ post-retreat Testimonial Consent process.

6
Social Media Tagging Policy
How We Reference You Online

The Tranquillity Retreats will not tag individual participants in any social media post without prior permission. If you would like to be tagged, you may opt in by informing us. Group photos posted to social media will not include individual name tags unless each person shown has provided consent.

7
Third-Party Sharing Restrictions
Who Has Access to Your Image

Media captured during the retreat will not be shared with, sold to, or licensed to any third party without your explicit consent. The only exception is professional editors and production personnel working under contract with The Tranquillity Retreats.

Data & Legal

8
Raw Media Retention
How Long We Keep Unedited Footage

Unedited raw footage and photographs will be retained for a maximum of 60 months (5 years) from the date of the retreat. After this period, all raw media not incorporated into published materials will be permanently deleted. Published materials may be retained and used indefinitely under the terms of this consent.

9
Participant-Captured Media
Your Own Photos and Videos

Participants are welcome to take personal photographs and videos during free time, meals, and general social moments. Photography and recording during facilitated sessions is not permitted unless specifically authorised by a facilitator.

10
Children and Minors
Additional Protections

No children or minors attend The Tranquillity Retreats. In the event that a participant is under 18 years of age, written consent from a parent or legal guardian is required in addition to this consent.

11
Revocation of Consent
Withdrawing Your Approval

You may revoke your consent to future use of your likeness by providing written notice to The Tranquillity Retreats. Revocation applies to future publications only; content already published in good faith prior to receiving your revocation notice is not subject to recall.

12
No Compensation
Use of Likeness

You acknowledge that you will receive no financial compensation for the use of your likeness under this consent. The retreat experience and its associated services constitute the full consideration for this consent.

13
Governing Law
Jurisdiction

This consent is governed by the laws of England and Wales. Any disputes arising from this consent shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

14
Modifications
Changes to This Consent

The Tranquillity Retreats reserves the right to update this Media Consent prior to the commencement of the retreat. Participants will be notified of any material changes and may withdraw their consent if they do not agree with the updated terms.

15
Contact
Questions About This Consent

If you have any questions about this Media Consent or wish to discuss any aspect of media capture during the retreat, please contact The Tranquillity Retreats team directly at retreats@thetranquillity.co.uk.

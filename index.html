Your Retreat Home

Panorama Hotel & Resort

Visit Hotel Website

Nestled on the coast of Sotra Island, just 40 minutes from Bergen, Panorama Hotel & Resort sits where the raw beauty of Western Norway meets quiet comfort. The hotel overlooks the fjord, framed by mountains and open sky. With its spa facilities, locally sourced dining, and the sound of the sea right outside, this will be your home for five transformational days.

Accommodation

Modern rooms with fjord or countryside views, heated floors, and premium bedding.

Spa & Wellness

Two outdoor hot tubs, warm water pools, cold plunge pool, steam bath, sauna, and floating sauna on the water. Free access for all guests.

Dining

Restaurant Vesterled serves seasonal, locally sourced cuisine. All retreat meals are vegetarian.

Getting There

44 km from Bergen Airport Flesland, approximately 40 minutes by car. Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.

Prepare

What to Pack

Mandatory Essentials

  • A yoga mat is required for sessions and is not provided by the hotel.
  • Non-slip footwear is essential for outdoor and on-site movement.
  • A warm over layer is required — Norway in October is cooler than expected.
  • A thin personal blanket or shawl is required for sessions and meditation — hotels in this region do not provide room blankets for use during sessions.

Clothing & Layers

  • Warm insulated jacket
  • Layering pieces (fleece, thermals)
  • Waterproof outer layer or rain jacket
  • Hiking boots or sturdy walking shoes
  • Hat and gloves (evenings get cold)
  • Scarf or neck warmer
  • Comfortable casual clothing for evenings
  • Thin personal blanket or shawl (for sessions and meditation)

Wellness & Practice

  • Yoga mat (required, not provided)
  • Comfortable clothing for sessions
  • Journal and pen
  • Swimwear (for the on-site spa)
  • Any personal meditation items
  • Water bottle

Travel Essentials

  • Valid passport
  • Travel insurance documentation
  • Any prescribed medication
  • Power adaptors and chargers (Type C/F plug)
  • A good book for the journey

Not Required

  • Towels (provided by the hotel)
  • Heavy work equipment (this is your time to unplug)

A note on the weather: October on the Norwegian coast can be unpredictable. Mornings may feel crisp and clear, only for rain to arrive by afternoon. Dress in layers you can add or remove, and always keep a waterproof layer close at hand.
